Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets on your website?

Booking tickets is easy! Just browse our events, select the one you want, choose your seats, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive an email confirmation with your e-tickets attached.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Some events may also offer cash payment options at the venue.

Can I cancel or refund my tickets?

Refund policies vary by event. Most tickets are non-refundable, but some events may allow cancellations up to 48 hours before the event. Please check the specific event's policy before booking.

How will I receive my tickets?

All tickets are delivered electronically via email immediately after purchase. You can either print them or show the QR code on your mobile device at the venue.

What if I lose my tickets?

Don't worry! You can always access your tickets by logging into your account or requesting a resend via email. Our support team can also help you retrieve your tickets.

Is there a booking fee?

Yes, we charge a small service fee per ticket to cover our platform costs. This fee is clearly displayed before you complete your purchase.

Can I transfer my tickets to someone else?

Yes, most tickets are transferable. You can forward the email with your e-tickets or use the "Transfer Tickets" option in your account dashboard.
