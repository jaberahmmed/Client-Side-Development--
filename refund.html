JMB Ticket Hub Refund Policy

Last Updated: 4 April 15, 2025

Important: Please read this refund policy carefully before purchasing tickets. By making a purchase, you agree to be bound by these terms.

1. General Refund Policy

Unless otherwise stated:

  • All ticket sales are final and non-refundable
  • Service fees and processing charges are non-refundable under any circumstances
  • Refunds, when applicable, will be issued to the original payment method
  • Refund processing may take 7-10 business days

2. Event Cancellations

If an event is cancelled and not rescheduled:

  • You will automatically receive a refund of the ticket face value
  • Service fees may not be refundable unless required by law
  • You will be notified via email with refund instructions
  • Refunds will be processed within 30 days of cancellation announcement

3. Postponed or Rescheduled Events

If an event is postponed or rescheduled:

  • Your tickets will typically remain valid for the new date
  • Refunds may be offered at the organizer's discretion if you cannot attend the new date
  • You must request a refund within 14 days of the reschedule announcement
  • Refund eligibility windows will be clearly communicated

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in these exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate purchases (must be reported within 24 hours)
  • Accidental purchases (must be reported immediately)
  • Event description significantly differs from what was advertised
  • When required by local consumer protection laws

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund (when eligible):

  1. Log in to your TicketHub account
  2. Go to "My Tickets" section
  3. Select the ticket(s) you wish to refund
  4. Click "Request Refund" and follow instructions
  5. Provide any required documentation

Alternatively, you may contact our customer support team with your order number.

6. Partial Refunds

In some cases, partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Partially cancelled events (e.g., multi-day festivals)
  • Seat relocations to less desirable locations
  • When only certain ticket types are eligible for refunds

7. Non-Refundable Items

The following are never eligible for refunds:

  • Service fees and processing charges
  • Donations or voluntary contributions
  • Gift cards or voucher purchases
  • Merchandise or concession items

8. Event Organizer Policies

Some events may have special refund policies set by the organizer:

  • These policies will override our standard policy
  • Special terms will be displayed during checkout
  • You are responsible for reviewing these before purchase

9. Force Majeure

TicketHub is not liable for refunds when events are cancelled due to circumstances beyond our control including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government actions
  • Pandemics or health emergencies
  • Acts of terrorism or war
